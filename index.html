Services

Dear Me services provide expert guidance for individuals facing emotional and mental challenges. Through evidence-based therapy, psychologists help clients understand and manage their thoughts and emotions, addressing issues like anxiety, depression, and relationship conflicts. These professionals offer a confidential space for personal growth and improved mental well-being. (AI Generated)

Psychotherapy

A therapeutic approach aimed at helping individuals explore and address their emotional, psychological, and behavioural challenges. Through various therapeutic techniques, such as Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavioural Therapy or Mindfulness, therapists work collaboratively with clients to identify and understand underlying issues, develop coping strategies , and promote personal growth and healing. Our licensed therapists specialize in a wide range of adult therapy services, including but not limited to:

  • Individual Therapy
  • Couple Therapy
  • Therapy for anxiety and depression
  • Grief Therapy
  • Social skill training
  • Therapy for alcohol/substance abuse

Behavioural Therapy

Behavioural therapists who work with children specialize in helping young individuals develop appropriate behaviours, improve social skills, and address emotional and behavioural challenges. Behavioural therapy includes

  • Personality Tests
  • Assessment for anxiety/ depression
  • Projective Tests
  • Autism Assessment
  • ADHD Assessment

Skill Training

Skill training in psychology refers to the process of developing and enhancing specific abilities, techniques, or behaviors that are relevant to the field of psychology. This training can encompass a wide range of skills, depending on the specialization and goals of the psychologist. Here are a few key aspects of skill training for psychologists:

  • Clinical Skills
  • Assessment Skills
  • Research Skills
  • Ethical and Legal Skills
  • Communication Skills
  • Cultural Competence
  • Supervision and Consultation

Counselling

Psychological counsellors offer a range of services to support individuals dealing with various mental and emotional challenges. These services are designed to help clients improve their mental well-being, cope with stressors, and develop healthier ways of thinking and behaving. Psychological counsellor can provide, crisis intervention, stress management anger management, health and wellness counselling.

Psychometric assessment

Psychometric assessments are standardised tests or tools used to measure and evaluate various psychological attributes, such as cognitive abilities, personality traits, and emotional well-being. Psychometric test aid in diagnosing mental health conditions and helps to develop more effective treatment plan.

  • Projective Tests
  • Personality Tests
  • Assessment for anxiety/ depression
  • Autism Assessment
  • ADHD Assessment
